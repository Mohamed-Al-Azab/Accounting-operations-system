# Accounting & Operations Management System

A web-based business system designed to support accounting workflows, operational follow-up, reporting, and daily management activities for service businesses.

## Key Features
- Daily sales and expense tracking
- Accounting workflow support
- Operational reporting
- Employee and branch follow-up
- Dashboard-based business monitoring
- Business process organization
